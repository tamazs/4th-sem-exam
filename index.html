Out of Sight

Out of Mind

For the last couple years, the number of large vessels lost at sea has steadily declined.
But the estimates of the number of shipwrecks currently untouched on the ocean floor are still high.
Many of them are left to degrade and leak contaminants into their environment.

Our Mission

Our mission is to shine a light on polluting shipwrecks that pose a threat to us environmentally, socially, economically, politically, and ethically. We wanted to tell a story of how governments covered roughly 2.5 to 20 million tons of oil, potentially leaking into the ocean causing untold damage to marine ecosystems, in bureaucratic red tape and regulations meant to protect the sanctity of sailor’s bodies long since eroded away by the elements. This film was conceptualized as a companion piece for an article on the subject, that specifically dives deeper into the extent of the problem and its effects on the North Atlantic and Arctic regions, written for JONAA; Journal of the North Atlantic and Arctic.
